# Kyoto Travel Guide

## Top Temples
- **Fushimi Inari Shrine**: Famous for thousands of red torii gates
- **Kinkaku-ji (Golden Pavilion)**: Stunning golden temple
- **Kiyomizu-dera**: Historic temple with wooden stage

## Best Time to Visit
Spring (March-May) for cherry blossoms or Fall (October-November) for autumn colors.

## Getting Around
Use the subway and buses. Get a day pass for unlimited rides.
